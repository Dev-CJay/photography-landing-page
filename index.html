    Our Humble Beginning

    In February 2024, We started as a small unit and primary basically on event coverage and outdoor sessions. We had very little hand on deck which caused a little delay to our growth. In time, we began massive production which led to our growth and expansion.

  • September 2024

    Our massive production gave us our major growth and as a result, we were able to set up our first studio and we had our first studio session which was beautiful.
